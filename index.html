Zekun Li

Ph.D. Student at University of Minnesota, Twin Cities

I am a computer science Ph.D. student at the University of Minnesota (UMN). My research interest is on the automatic understanding of historical maps with computer vision techniques. I have worked on text detection of historical map labels, connecting separated text labels, linking recognized place names to existing knowledge bases (entity linking) and label type inference (entity typing). Previously at the University of Southern California (USC), I also worked on object detection projects in the Information Sciences Institute (ISI) and Spatial Sciences Institute (SSI).
